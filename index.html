The Concept

Sound has depth.

Standard DAW panners give you one axis: left or right. CleverPan adds a second — near and far. Place your sound on a 2D canvas and watch the stereo field react: the perspective grid shifts, the focal compression changes, the depth filters and attenuates like real acoustic space.

Les panners classiques n'offrent qu'un seul axe : gauche ou droite. CleverPan en ajoute un second — proche ou loin. Placez votre son sur une surface 2D et observez le champ stéréo réagir : la grille de perspective se déplace, la compression focale change, la profondeur filtre et atténue comme dans un vrai espace acoustique.

🎬
FOCAL PRESETS

Wide Shot, Medium Shot, Tight Shot, Close-Up. Each preset compresses the stereo field like a zoom lens — from immersive wide-angle to near-mono close-up.

Les focales cinématographiques compressent le champ stéréo.

📐
DEPTH AXIS

NEAR to FAR. Depth drives a low-pass filter and volume attenuation simultaneously — simulating air absorption and distance in one gesture.

L'axe de profondeur simule la distance acoustique.

↔️
DIVERGENCE

AUTO mode ties divergence to depth. MANUAL mode decouples them — place a far sound in wide stereo, or a close sound in near-mono. A classic cinematic trick.

Découpler la divergence de la profondeur.

🎯
TRACK MODE

When active, the perspective vanishing point follows your sound source at 50% — like a camera turning its head toward the subject.

Le point de fuite suit le sujet.
